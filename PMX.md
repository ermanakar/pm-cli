# ProdMaster CLI

## Vision

A cutting-edge CLI tool leveraging AI to streamline product management for founders and engineers.

## Target Audience

Tech-savvy product managers and engineering founders

## Tech Stack

TypeScript, React (with Ink), Node.js, OpenAI GPT API

## Core Features

- AI-powered task automation and insights for product management
- Component-based CLI interface offering an efficient workflow
- Modular design for easy extension and maintenance
- Integration with OpenAI GPT for intelligent product management support
- Lightweight and flexible data management without conventional databases

## Domain

Product Management

## Context

ProdMaster CLI is designed as a modern command-line interface tool that brings the sophistication of artificial intelligence to product management tasks. It uses TypeScript and React (with Ink) for a component-based CLI experience, and Node.js for backend logic. The integration of OpenAI's GPT API allows it to provide intelligent support and insights, making product management tasks more efficient for its users. Aimed at tech-savvy founders and product managers, ProdMaster CLI represents a blend of innovation, ease of use, and efficiency, designed to improve the workflow of product management without the overhead of traditional databases or complex interfaces.
